# 🥛 DairyFlow AI

### _An Explainable AI Copilot for Dairy Supply Chain Intelligence_

> _"What if your supply chain dashboard didn't just show numbers—but actually explained what they mean and what to do next?"_

DairyFlow AI is an AI-powered decision intelligence platform that combines **hierarchical demand forecasting**, **inventory analytics**, and **natural language reasoning** to help dairy supply chain managers make faster, explainable, and data-driven decisions.

Unlike traditional dashboards, DairyFlow doesn't stop at visualizing data—it predicts future demand, quantifies inventory risk, recommends replenishment actions, and explains every recommendation in natural language.

---

# 🚀 Why DairyFlow AI?

Managing a dairy supply chain is a constant balancing act.

Produce too little, and retailers face stock-outs.

Produce too much, and highly perishable products become waste.

Most organizations already have production, inventory, and sales dashboards—but managers still spend hours manually interpreting reports before making decisions.

DairyFlow AI bridges this gap by acting as an **AI Supply Chain Copilot** that transforms operational data into clear, explainable business recommendations.

---

# 📌 Problem Statement

Dairy supply chains generate large volumes of production, sales, and inventory data, yet operational decisions are still largely manual. Due to the perishable nature of dairy products, inaccurate demand estimation often results in stock-outs, overproduction, and unnecessary spoilage.

The objective of DairyFlow AI is to build an explainable AI assistant that predicts demand, evaluates inventory risk, and answers natural-language business questions using data-driven reasoning.
